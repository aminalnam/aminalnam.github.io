Privacy Policy for CosNFX

Last updated: 21 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the CosNFX mobile application ("CosNFX", "the app", "we") handles your information. CosNFX is a sound-effects soundboard application.

Summary

CosNFX does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device and does not require an account, an internet connection, or any sign-in to function.

Information we collect

We do not collect any personal or sensitive user data. CosNFX does not gather analytics, usage statistics, device identifiers, location data, contacts, or any other information about you or your device. No data is sent to us or to any third party.

Permissions

CosNFX only uses the permissions required to play audio on your device. It does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or storage for the purpose of collecting data.

Advertising

CosNFX does not contain third-party advertising and does not use any advertising or tracking SDKs.

Children's privacy

CosNFX is intended for users aged 13 and older. Because the app collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children.

Third-party services

CosNFX does not integrate any third-party services that collect user data.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the developer at cosNFX@jonathancapone.com.
