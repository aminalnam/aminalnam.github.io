Overview

Bring Your Character to Life

CosNFX is an app designed to bring your Cosplays, LARP characters, and Halloween costumes to life. It makes your costume interactive by using your physical arm movements to trigger sound effects—so when you throw a punch, cast a spell, or strike a pose, the audio perfectly matches your motion.

It gives you complete creative control to build your own character profile and upload your own sounds. You can create your own custom physical gestures and link them together to perform multipart combo moves that trigger epic sound sequences.

For a louder and more immersive experience, the companion phone software plays sound effects simultaneously with your watch. You can even interact with other CosNFX users and actually battle each other!
