Biography

I am an artist and creator focused on connecting physical hardware to the digital experiences built on top of it. From building decentralized mesh networks in marine environments to designing wearable controllers that sense movement, my work combines creative exploration, tight data limits, and interfaces that are easy to use.

This portfolio serves as an honest, after-the-fact record of the real problems I ran into when bringing complex interactive systems from concept to reality.
