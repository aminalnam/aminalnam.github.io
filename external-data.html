Orbital Mechanics

Satellite Pass Tracking.

The gateway/satellites.py module integrates directly with orbital tracking APIs. Because surface edge nodes often lack cellular coverage, they must dump their high-bandwidth video data to passing low-earth orbit (LEO) satellites. The gateway calculates the exact orbital windows and commands the edge node to align its directional antennas just before the satellite breaks the horizon.

Extreme Weather

National Hurricane Center.

The gateway/nhc.py and gateway/weather.py modules ingest raw GRIB models and cyclone probability cones. If the gateway mathematically determines that a deployed asset intersects with the 48-hour probability cone of a major storm, it automatically triggers an automatic power-down sequence, commanding the edge node to dive or return to base.
