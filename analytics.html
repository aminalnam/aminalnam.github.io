Time-Series Processing

Gateway Analytics Engine.

The gateway/analytics.py engine is the mathematical workhorse of the shore system. It constantly crunches millions of historical SQLite telemetry points. By utilizing numpy and scipy, it generates moving averages, detects anomalous sensor drift, and pre-computes spatial statistics.

The Mathematics: When analyzing marine telemetry (like Salinity or pH), rogue acoustic spikes are inevitable due to biological fouling or sensor glitches. Standard averages are destroyed by these massive outliers. OMEGA utilizes the QARTOD (Quality Assurance / Quality Control of Real-Time Oceanographic Data) standards, calculating a robust median absolute deviation (MAD) instead of a standard standard-deviation.

Execution: The numpy arrays execute vectorized operations to instantly threshold incoming data bundles. If a sensor value exceeds 3 robust-sigmas, it is mathematically flagged as "Suspect". At 6 sigmas, it is flagged as "Fail" and stripped from the active mapping visualization, protecting the operators from reacting to ghost data.

gateway/qc.py
def _threshold_bundle(values: list[float]) -> dict[str, float] | None:
    # Calculate QARTOD compliant statistical bounds
    center = np.median(values)
    mad = np.median(np.abs(values - center))
    robust_sigma = mad * 1.4826
    
    return {
        "gross_range_suspect_min": center - robust_sigma * 3,
        "gross_range_suspect_max": center + robust_sigma * 3,
        "gross_range_fail_min": center - robust_sigma * 6,
        "gross_range_fail_max": center + robust_sigma * 6,
    }
Depth Mapping

Bathymetry Pipelines.

The gateway/bathymetry_ingest.py and bathymetry_export.py modules handle massive acoustic sounding files (CSV). The pipeline normalizes millions of overlapping sonar pings, filters out acoustic multipath errors, and passes the clean data into the 3D Inverse Distance Weighting (IDW) interpolation engine, creating the continuous ocean floor topology.

Predictive Maintenance

Fast Fourier Transforms (FFT).

Wait until a $5,000 thruster physically breaks in the middle of the ocean, and the mission is already lost. OMEGA utilizes predictive acoustic analytics to detect mechanical failure weeks before the hardware actually snaps.

Vibration Matrix

Raw Ingestion

The edge node records 20Hz vibration data from the IMU mounted directly to the propulsion housing.

Spectral Shift

FFT Processing

The gateway converts the time-domain vibration into the frequency domain. It detects microscopic shifts in the harmonic frequency of the spinning propeller.

Bearing Failure

Anomaly Alert

A sudden spike in the 120Hz harmonic band mathematically indicates a failing ceramic bearing, triggering an automatic Return-To-Base (RTB).
