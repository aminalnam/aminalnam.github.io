Project 2

BETA Framework Modules

⚙️

Deterministic Fuzzing

A relentless evaluation engine. Continuously generates thousands of highly complex synthetic payload injections, simulating multiple drifting fleets, acoustic anomalies, and deliberate hardware packet corruption to stress-test the OMEGA core.
📈

High-Frequency Analytics

Calculates extreme-scale telemetry metrics in real-time. Establishes dynamic 5-minute rolling P95 latency baselines and strictly enforces SLAs across the entire DTN routing pipeline.
🤖

AI Remediation Ticketing

Fully automated CI/CD integration. When the fuzzer breaks the OMEGA architecture, the AI Core instantly synthesizes error logs, identifies the exact Python/C++ regression, and pushes Jira-style remediation tickets to engineering.

Open Source Geospatial

Acoustic Bathymetric Mapping

Advanced Python algorithms implementing Inverse Distance Weighting (IDW) interpolation. Converts sparse, noisy acoustic pings into stunning, highly-accurate topographic depth contours and Google Earth KML overlays.
