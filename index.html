Oceanic Measurement & Environmental Geospatial Array

OMEGA is a massive, unified marine data system designed for extreme environmental edge-computing. What began as an Arduino logger has evolved into a massively distributed platform encompassing autonomous fleets, resilient mesh networking, and predictive digital twins.
