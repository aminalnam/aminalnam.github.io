Environmental Mapping Featured

Oceanic Geospatial Measurement System

A mobile bathymetric survey system built around a small ROV platform. The system records depth, position, and environmental data, filters measurements in real time, and reconstructs terrain surfaces through interpolation. The project includes embedded hardware, logging logic, mapping scripts, contour generation, and Google Earth overlays.
