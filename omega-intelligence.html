Edge AI

Machine Learning Inferencing (TinyML).

Future fleet iterations will run TinyML models directly on the ESP32 edge nodes. By training a TensorFlow Lite model on baseline vibration profiles, the node will be able to detect motor anomalies (e.g., kelp wrapped around a propeller) and transmit an alarm before critical failure occurs.
